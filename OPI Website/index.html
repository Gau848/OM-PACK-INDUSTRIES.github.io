    Manufacturing Process :

    We have a set of 7 machines, to perform a production of boxes and related corrugated products.

    Corrugation is a fairly simple process that makes use of minimum components such as the fluting paper, liner paper and glue to bind them both. There are two rolls, one with the fluting paper and one with the liner paper. Liner paper is a simple paper. The paper from the fluting roll is passed through corrugating rolls which flutes it. This moves forward and glue is applied on the peaks of the paper (usually it is starch based adhesive).
